Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our mining plans

What is the minimum contract period?
There's no minimum contract period. You can cancel your mining plan at any time. Plans are billed monthly, and you can switch between plans as your needs change.
How are the mining rewards calculated?
Mining rewards are calculated based on the hash rate of your plan, current network difficulty, and the price of HyperCoin. Our dashboard provides real-time estimates of your expected earnings.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit cards, PayPal, and various cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and of course, HyperCoin. All payments are secure and encrypted.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new rate takes effect immediately. When downgrading, the new rate applies at the start of your next billing cycle.
